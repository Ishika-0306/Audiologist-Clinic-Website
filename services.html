Our Services

Hearing Tests

Comprehensive hearing evaluations using state-of-the-art technology to identify hearing loss and other auditory issues.Hearing tests check a person's ability to hear the loudness and pitch of sounds. The results are charted on a graph (audiogram) to help pinpoint the severity and causes of hearing problems.

Hearing Aid Fitting

Personalized fitting and tuning of hearing aids to ensure comfort, clarity, and effective sound amplification.A hearing aid fitting is when you try on your new hearing aids at your hearing care clinic. Your provider will run tests and calibrations to make sure the hearing aids are customized to your hearing loss.

Earwax Removal

Safe and professional removal of earwax that may be causing hearing blockage or discomfort.The insert this carefully into your ear and scoop out the softened earwax, removing it entirely from the canal.Your health care provider can remove excess wax by using a small, curved tool called a curet or by using suction techniques.

Tinnitus Management

Effective strategies and treatments to manage ringing or buzzing in the ears caused by tinnitus.For many people, tinnitus sound therapy can be very effective in decreasing how noticeable or bothersome your tinnitus is over time.Effective strategies and treatments to manage ringing or buzzing in the ears caused by tinnitus.

Custom Ear Protection

Custom ear protection or custom earmolds are devices that offer preventative care, helping you take care of your ears when you engage in activities that have a potential to cause temporary or permanent damage to your ears or hearing.Hearing protection devices decrease the intensity of sound that reaches the eardrum.

Pediatric Audiology
